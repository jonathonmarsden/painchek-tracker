What Is This?

PainChek Tracker is a privacy-first web app for tracking your PCK (PainChek Ltd - ASX:PCK) stock portfolio. It runs entirely in your browser - no servers, no accounts, no data sharing.

What it does:

  • • Tracks your purchases (tranches)
  • • Calculates gains/losses automatically
  • • Shows live prices (15-20 min delay)
  • • Displays ASX announcements
  • • Provides portfolio charts

What it does NOT do:

  • • Tell you when to buy/sell
  • • Predict future prices
  • • Provide investment advice
  • • Share your data with anyone
  • • Require registration/login

Getting Started

Step 1: Open the App

Visit: painchek-tracker.netlify.app

Step 2: Add Your First Tranche

  1. Click "Add Tranche" button
  2. Enter purchase date (required)
  3. Enter number of shares (required)
  4. Enter purchase price (or leave blank for auto-lookup)
  5. Click "Add Tranche"

Step 3: Add to Home Screen (Mobile)

iPhone/iPad (Safari):

  1. Tap Share button (up arrow)
  2. Tap "Add to Home Screen"
  3. Tap "Add"

Android (Chrome):

  1. Tap menu (three dots)
  2. Tap "Add to Home screen"
  3. Tap "Add"

Key Features

What is a "Tranche"?

A tranche is a single purchase batch. If you bought PCK shares on different dates or prices, track each separately.

Sept 15: 1,000 shares @ $0.235 → Tranche 1

Oct 1: 500 shares @ $0.242 → Tranche 2

Why? See which purchases were profitable and track your average cost basis.

Quick Stats Panel

Total Shares: Combined holdings
Avg Cost Basis: Average price paid
Break-Even Price: Price to reach profitability
Days Held: Since first purchase

Price Alerts

Set high/low price targets. Get notified when you next open the app (not real-time push notifications).

CSV Export

Download your portfolio data for backup or tax reporting. Includes all tranches with gains/losses.

Understanding Charts

Portfolio Performance Chart

What it shows: Portfolio value (blue) vs. invested amount (grey dashed)

Making Money

Blue above grey = profit

Losing Money

Blue below grey = loss

Break Even

Lines touch = no gain/loss

Why it matters: See if your investment strategy is working over time.

Price Chart (1 Year)

What it shows: PCK stock price over last 12 months

  • • Upward slope = Bull market (rising prices)
  • • Downward slope = Bear market (falling prices)
  • • Flat = Consolidation (sideways movement)

Why it matters: Spot trends and potential entry/exit points.

Volume Chart

What it shows: Number of shares traded each day

High Volume + Price Up

Strong buying (bullish)

High Volume + Price Down

Panic selling (bearish)

Pro tip: Volume spikes often precede ASX announcements. Check "Recent Announcements" when you see unusual activity.

Privacy & Security

100% Client-Side Storage

All portfolio data stays on YOUR device using browser localStorage. Nothing is sent to servers, cloud, or databases.

What's Stored Locally:

  • • Your tranches (dates, shares, prices)
  • • Your price alerts
  • • App preferences

What's Transmitted:

  • • Stock symbol "PCK.AX" (for prices)
  • • Company code "PCK" (for news)
  • • NO personal data

Important: If you clear browser data, your portfolio is deleted. Export to CSV regularly as backup!

Troubleshooting

Unable to load stock data

Causes: Yahoo Finance API down, network issue, or CORS proxy offline

Fix: Click "Try Again" button, wait a few minutes, or hard refresh (Ctrl+Shift+R)

Charts not showing

Causes: Chart.js failed to load or browser compatibility

Fix: Hard refresh (Ctrl+Shift+R), update browser, or try Chrome/Firefox

Price alerts not working

This is not a bug! Alerts only check when you open the app (not real-time push notifications). Check the app daily if you have active alerts.

Data lost after browser update

Cause: Browser update cleared localStorage (rare)

Prevention: Export to CSV monthly and save to cloud storage

FAQ

Does data sync across devices?

No. Each device has its own localStorage. Export CSV on one device and manually re-enter on another.

Why are prices delayed?

ASX exchange rules require 15-20 minute delays for free data feeds. Real-time data requires expensive subscriptions.

Can I track other stocks?

No, this app is specifically built for PCK. You'd need to modify the source code.

Can I use this for tax purposes?

The CSV export is accurate (dates, shares, prices, gains/losses) but always double-check and consult your accountant.

Is there a mobile app?

No native apps, but you can add the web app to your home screen on iOS/Android. It behaves like a native app!
